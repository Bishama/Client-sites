A NOTE FROM
THE FOUNDER

A cozy and well-furnished home is everyone's dream. The Feather Company was founded to bring more comfort to your life through premium, accessible essentials for your home.

As a design enthusiast and super consumer of premium home goods, I couldn't find a brand with high quality and accessible home accessories. Soon, I noticed a gap in the market and saw a genuine business opportunity. I launched Feather Company in 2018 as an online-only direct-to-consumer brand focused on creating an impeccable bedding experience.

We have created a loyal community dedicated to comfort. We're committed to providing you with exceptional everyday home essentials that make your home comfortable and elegant.
